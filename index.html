General Configuration

Connectable is RAM-only and resets to ON at every power-up. Turn OFF to ship a pure non-connectable beacon; power-cycle the device to make it connectable again.
BLE address is fixed (factory FICR address of the chip) and cannot be changed.

Slot Configuration

1. Mở nRF Connect, đặt điện thoại sát beacon (~0 m). 2. Đọc RSSI (dBm) của thiết bị này. 3. Nhập vào ô dưới → Apply → bấm "Write slot" để lưu. Firmware tự quy đổi: iBeacon dùng giá trị −41 dB, Eddystone dùng thẳng.
Encodes scheme + suffix per Eddystone spec, max 17 bytes after the scheme.
TLM auto-generated: VDD voltage, die temperature, adv counter, uptime.
AD type 0xFF (Manufacturer Specific Data). Default company 0x5054 = Tapama.
Advanced: you build the entire AD yourself. Malformed lengths = the slot won't advertise. Spaces are ignored.

Voltage is read live from the device (chip VDD). Level maps 3.0 V = 100%, 2.0 V = 0% (3 V lithium). Remaining time = (level × capacity) ÷ estimated current — a "good enough" figure, not a measured one. Assumes the cell powers the nRF directly (no regulator). For exact figures use a power profiler.
